Common questions

Clear answers about how we work, what to expect, and how we measure success in SEO projects.

How long do technical SEO fixes typically take?

Most technical fixes are implemented within 2-4 weeks, depending on site complexity and developer availability. Critical issues like indexing blocks or crawl errors are prioritized first. We provide a detailed timeline after the initial audit, breaking work into phases so you see progress quickly.

What access do you need to my website and tools?

We require read-only access to Google Search Console and Analytics for diagnostics. For technical implementations, we need FTP/hosting access or collaboration with your development team. All access is handled securely, and we provide clear documentation of what we need and why.

How do you diagnose Google indexing issues?

We start with a comprehensive crawl using industry tools, then cross-reference with Search Console data to identify patterns. Common culprits include robots.txt blocks, noindex tags, canonical errors, or server response issues. We document every finding with screenshots and actionable fixes.

How are SEO results measured and reported?

We track organic traffic, keyword rankings, indexation status, and conversion metrics monthly. You receive a dashboard showing progress against baseline metrics, plus detailed reports explaining what changed and why. We focus on business outcomes—not just vanity metrics like traffic spikes.

Do you work directly with development teams?

Yes, we collaborate closely with your developers or hosting provider. We provide clear technical specifications, code snippets, and implementation guides. If your team has questions, we jump on calls to clarify requirements. Many clients appreciate that we speak both SEO and developer language.

What's the communication and reporting cadence?

You receive weekly progress updates during active implementation phases, and monthly performance reports once optimizations are live. We're available via email or WhatsApp for urgent issues. Every report includes next steps, so you always know what's happening and what's coming next.
